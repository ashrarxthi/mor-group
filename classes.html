About each class

What to expect

Kathak Foundations
Sundays · 10:00 am
with Aishwarya R.

Learn the core vocabulary of Kathak — footwork, spins, hand gestures (mudras), and introductory compositions. No prior experience needed.

Beginner · Open Level
Bharatanatyam Basics
Wednesdays · 6:30 pm
with Shrinali P.

An introduction to Bharatanatyam — adavus, hand gestures, eye movements, and the foundational postures of this ancient South Indian form.

Beginner
Semi-Classical Fusion
Tuesdays · 7:00 pm
with Shrinali P.

Classical technique meets expressive storytelling. The perfect bridge between tradition and contemporary feeling. Open to all levels.

All Levels
Kathak Intensive
Thursdays · 7:00 pm
with Aishwarya R.

Deeper study of taal, layakari, and abhinaya for students who want to go further. Some prior Kathak experience recommended.

Intermediate
Bollywood Fusion
Saturdays · 11:00 am
with Shrinali P.

High-energy choreography rooted in classical form. A great entry point for those new to Indian dance styles. Fun, upbeat, welcoming.

All Levels
Little Peacocks
Saturdays · 9:30 am
with Aishwarya & Shrinali

A joyful introduction to Indian classical dance for young movers. Rhythm, coordination, storytelling, and the joy of moving together.

Ages 6–14
